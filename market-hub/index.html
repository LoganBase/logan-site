How to use this dashboard
What is this?

Market Hub is a systematic macro framework that scores market conditions across 10 dimensions. Each card scores Bullish, Neutral, or Bearish. The executive score at the top aggregates all 10 cards into a single market posture.

Reading the score
ScorePostureMeaning
8–10Risk-OnBroad-based bullish conditions across most dimensions
6–7CautiousMixed signals — some tailwinds, some headwinds
0–5Risk-OffMajority of indicators showing deterioration
The 10 cards
#CardWhat it measures
01RegimeSPY vs 200d SMA — is the market in a secular bull or bear?

08CommoditiesUSCI, copper, gold, energy — are real assets confirming growth?
09EquitiesThematic watchlist — how many names are above key MAs?
10CreditHYG, LQD, JNK — is the credit market signalling stress?
Deep dives

Click any card to open its deep dive — historical charts, stat boxes, and a range selector (1WK to 10Y). Use ❖ Generate AI Summary to get a 2-3 sentence analyst interpretation of the current data. Summaries are cached daily so repeat clicks are instant.

Refresh button

Pulls today's closing prices from Yahoo Finance into the database and reloads the dashboard. Best used after 4pm ET when markets close. The dashboard also auto-refreshes nightly at 10pm UTC.
